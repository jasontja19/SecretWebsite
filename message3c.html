99 reasons for me to give up.

99 to walk away and move on.

1 reason for me to stay.

And I chose to stay.

Because I love you.

So when you asked me, “Why are you okay with being my friend?”

Do I really need another reason?

Even after what happened during Christmas,

not once did I give up on you.

It hurted... a lot.

But it was never enough to make me walk away.

And if this is what you call “stupid” or “weak,”

then yes... I am.

And I'm proud of it.
